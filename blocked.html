About Phishing

What is Phishing? Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers impersonate legitimate organizations to steal sensitive information such as passwords, credit card numbers, or personal data.

Common Harms: Credential theft, financial loss, identity theft, malware infection, and unauthorized access to your accounts.

Tips to Recognize Phishing Links:

  • Check for misspellings or strange characters in the URL.
  • Look for suspicious subdomains or IP addresses instead of domain names.
  • Be cautious of urgent or threatening language in emails or websites.
  • Verify the website's security certificate (look for HTTPS and a padlock icon).
  • Do not click on links from unknown or unexpected sources.
